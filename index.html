Music

Echoes of Goodbye

“Echoes of Goodbye” is a pensive song that tells a story of love lost and the melancholy recollections that come with it. This composition creates the essence of a room empty yet full with memories of the darling,
heard only through the echoes of their name that have no end. The captivating tune, after telling a story of love and bliss quickly brought to an end, accentuates these melancholy feelings,
showing the difficulty one goes through whilst trying to let go and move on. It clarifies the point that even as the memory of a love-filled past fades, some of it is stored in the living heart as echoes.

Upcoming: Gravity

The narrative depicted in the song 'Gravity' manages to capture the essence of being in love. A peculiar body of attraction reunites the narrator with a certain special one.
These lines demonstrate a sense of inevitability, and they juxtapose the power of love and levity together. In the course of the song, the notions come full-circle,
and we realize that one cannot argue but admit love is truly overpowering. Thus, love is made grand in "Gravity" and lets us know to fall for someone is, actually, a good thing to do.
